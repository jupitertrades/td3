How Advertising Revenue Sharing Works



Why Jupes?


  • + Fairer Reward for Affiliates. The industry standard in affiliate marketing is to reward the last click that generates the sale while ignoring the contributions of others that helped drive users to conversion. By awarding Jupes based on unique visitors SixJupiter helps affiliates at all points in the conversion funnel.
  • + Standardizing Crypto Affiliate Programs. Cryptocurrency affiliate programs from brokers typically pay out in Ethereum, Bitcoin, or even another token. SixJupiter standardizes all these into Jupes and allows Jupes to be redeemed for traditional nation-state currencies, which makes affiliate program management easier for publishers so that they can focus on what they do best: publishing great content.

    • The fine print


    • + Calculating Your Unique Visitor Percentage. SixJupiter tracks the number of unique visitors that each publisher shows SixJupiter's ads and content to. SixJupiter does this by installing a Google Analytics snippet on the web pages it shares to your site.
    • + Monthly Jupes Minting. Each month, SixJupiter mints 100 Jupes, its cryptocurrency. Jupes supply and transactions can be observed on Etherscan.
    • + Jupes Distributed Based on Unique Visitor Percentage. SixJupiter keeps 50 Jupes to fund its operations and reward its core team members. The remaining 50 Jupes are distributed proportionally based on how many unique visitors they showed SixJupiter's content to. So, if Property A is responsible for 18% of all unique visitors that saw SixJupiter content in a given month, Property A will have 9 Jupes deposited into its wallet for that month. Unique visitor data and revenue will be shared with members of our network via our Slack channel, where publishers can also engage with each other, our core team, and observe our operating processes in their entirety.
    • + SixJupiter Will Always Buy Jupes. SixJupiter will buy Jupes in exchange for US dollars from any holder of them who has acquired Jupes through non-fraudulent means. The amount SixJupiter will pay is based on the amount of US dollars in its revenue pool divided by the outstanding supply of Jupes, thus creating a price floor for Jupes. Any Jupes purchased are instantly burned, thus reducing the overall supply of Jupes and helping to further support its value. The rate SixJupiter will pay is updated daily on SixJupiter.com. Prior to requesting a conversion of Jupes into US dollars, holders must complete a W9 form, so that SixJupiter can remain in compliance with United States' tax authorities. To start a conversion process, simply message us to initiate an official request.
