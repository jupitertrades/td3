Widgets to Capture Crypto Traffic and Leads

Example Widget: Bitcoin Velocity Index

  • + The example Bitcoin Velocity Index widget shows how much Bitcoin is being used for transactions and trading versus simply holding as a wealth asset relative to its money supply.
  • + The index is calculated by taking the sum of the past 7 days worth of Bitcoin traded on the blockchain and dividing it by the average of Bitcoin money supply over that period.
  • + Monetary velocity is of interest for traders who believe more valuable currencies have a lower velocity and are used as stores of wealth rather than transactional currencies. (see Gresham's Law)
  • + SixJupiter can work with you to create a landing page for your widget, which can feature a signup form allowing users to get alerts email and/or SMS to the index, and thus can be a lead capture source for you.
