The details


  • + Data collection. We collect data from the Ethereum blockchain, various data providers (CoinGecko, Etherscan, Ethplorer, Cryptocompare, Twitter) to develop a comprehensive dataset for over 1,000 cryptocurrencies on the Ethereum blockchain. Take a look at the dataset we build daily.
  • + Content generation. Our artificial intelligence takes this data set and generates text reports, complete with interactive visualizations and relevant ads, for each of the 1,000+ Ethereum tokens in our index.
  • + Seamless integration into your web site. Reports are posted using the WordPress built into your site, so that they seamlessly integrate into the look, feel, navigation, and administration console of your web site.
  • + Get paid! You keep 100% of the banner ad revenue surrounding the content SixJupiter creates for your site. Revenue from ads inserted by SixJupiter are shared with you monthly in the form of our cryptocurrency, Jupes, which are deposited into the Ethereum wallet you specify monthly. Jupes help all the members of the SixJupiter network profit together while also providing the safety of being able to converted into US dollars at any time.
