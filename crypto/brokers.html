How it Works


The process to getting started with utilizing a SixJupiter chatbot for your business as follows:
  1. Complete our onboarding form. This helps us get to know your firm and your needs, so that we can build the best possible bot for you.
  2. After receiving your onboarding form, a representative from our firm will contact you to discuss details and plan the development of your chatbot. We'll ask you for as much information as you can provide your product that you'd like customers to know, as well as any questions you find are frequently asked. This will give us a foundation to start building an intelligent bot that understands your offering.
  3. You'll be provided with a javascript code to embed your chat form wherever you like, with 24/7 availability. If you'd like, questions our bot cannot answer will go to an application where a human member of your team can read and respond, creating a seamless user experience.
  4. We will continue to work with you to build a bot that learns about your customers and your offerings, so that it can offer your customers personalized service that makes the most of your products.
  5. Billing is a combination of a fixed monthly fee and a per message fee. Our goal is to help you dramatically reduce your sales and service costs, while helping you delight your customers by delivering them a bot capable of providing intelligent and personalized service.
