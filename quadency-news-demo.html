August 15, 2019: Majors Down 8.3%, as Bitcoin Crosses 20 Day Moving Average; PHX Rallies By Nearly 140%

How Did the Majors Fare?

The five major cryptocurrencies -- Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, and Ripple -- collectively were down 8.3%, on a market cap weighted basis. This was on above average volume, with volume 58.47% greater than than its 7 day average. In particular, the currency that fared best, Bitcoin, dropped 7.73%, while the worst performer of the bunch, Ripple, dropped 11.47% day over day. Ultimately, none of the major cryptocurrencies were up for the day. On a relative basis, the market dominance -- meaning the percent of total crypto market capitalization belonging to the coin in question -- has been trending up for Bitcoin, which now accounts for approximately 68.06% of the total crypto market. Crypto market dominance has been trending down over the past two weeks for Ethereum, Ripple and Litecoin, which now make up about 7.5%, 4.35% and 1.81% of the total crypto market.

Notable Price Action in the Majors

  • Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin and Ripple have fallen now for 3 consecutive days.
  • Bitcoin Cash crossed below its 5, 10, and 20 day moving averages.
  • Bitcoin crossed below its 20 day moving average.
